The factors included mixing amount of used sodium silicate sand, particle size of coal gangue, sintering temperature and holding time. And the preparation mechanism of the permeable fired paving bricks based on used sodium silicate sands was analyzed by micro-structure. The results show that the permeability coefficient and compressive strength of permeable fired paving bricks based on used sodium silicate sands can meet the requirements of national standards. The combination property of permeable fired paving bricks is better when the mixing amount of used sodium silicate sand is 70%. The permeability coefficient and compressive strength of permeable fired paving bricks could reach the requirements of national standards when the particle size of coal gangue is 0.16~2.5 mm and the sinteringtemperature achieves 1 000°C. And the suitable holding time is 40~60 min.Key words: used sodium silicate sands; permeable fired paving bricks; permeability coefficient; compressive strength; microstructure; preparation technology   0 引言随着中国城镇化建设的发展,许多城镇地表被 越来越多的混凝土所覆盖,地表“硬化”引发了 “城市热岛”效应.新型环保材料透水砖作为新型 功能性道路铺装材料,具有其他砖所不具有的特性,主要是具有良好的透水性和保湿性,可以保证 大地与大气之间的水汽和热能的交换;同时,透水 砖也是有效利用雨水资源的重要途径.因此,透水砖 可以很好的遏制“城市热岛”效应[1-2].  近年来,国内外研究人员采用工业固废制备环保型透水砖,主要有透水烧结路面砖和透水混凝土 收稿日期:2015-10-21基金项目:河南省科技攻关基金项目(102102210471) 作者简介:王继娜(1982-),女,河南洛阳人,博士,副教授,主要从事绿色建筑材料研究. 本文编校:杨芳辽宁工程技术大学学报(自然科学版)网址:http://202.199.224.158/ http://xuebao.lntu.edu.cn/
