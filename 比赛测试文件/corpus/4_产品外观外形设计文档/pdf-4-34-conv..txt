It analyzes the methods of extracting and exchanging the specific images of Meishan totems, such as cit- ing the totem images directly, collaging or deconstructing the totem graphic symbols, disassembling and reconstituting the food packaging construction to the totem form, and so on. It expounds emphatically extracting and reappearing the totem cultural connotation by metaphor and symbolism. The method of metaphor discusses three totem forms how to apply to the local food packaging design, that the metaphorical objects respectively are concrete objects, abstract spirit and events with longitudinal time. And the method of symbolism researches two groups application modes to put the totem culture into food packaging, which are monosemantic and polysemantic symbolism, unconscious and conscious symbolism. It improves the cultural and artistic quality of local specialty food packaging design from graphisme to structure, sets up their brand images, promotes consumption, and spreads Meishan culture with the point of penetration of totem culture and its images. KEY WORDS: Meishan region; totem culture; food packaging梅山地区指湖南中部以安化、新化两县为中心, 辐射至邵阳、娄底、冷水江等县市。当地地势险峻、 语言侏离,故“旧不与中国通”。至公元 1072 年,蔡 煜、章敦开梅山置新化、安化两县,此地区才吸收中 原地区儒、释、道等主流文化。但此地仍以蚩尤为祖 先,承袭了大量远古巫傩文化(如图腾崇拜)。受气 候、地理、历史、民俗文化等影响,当地饮食独具特色,是其重要旅游纪念品之一。但目前市场上大部分 梅山特色食品包装制作粗糙、设计缺乏文化内涵,既 无法体现当地深厚的文化底韵,又难以引起游客的购 买兴趣。近年信息化高度发展,当地的食品生产者既 看到现代化设计的先进,又初步意识到传承传统文化 的重要,对食品包装风格与模式的取舍处于无所适从 的状态,因此,这里以当地独特的图腾文化为切入点, 收稿日期:2017-02-16 作者简介:李川(1984―),女,湖南人,博士生,主攻建筑与环境艺术设计。 通讯作者:陈飞虎(1954―),男,湖南人,湖南大学教授,主要研究方向为环境艺术设计。
