The discussions focused on i) Its increasingly wide applica- tions,as a good semiconductor material in fabrication of solar cells,photo-electronics devices and sensors,cladding materials for fusion reactor,and nuclear radiation detection because of its capability of working in environments of high-temperature,high frequency,high-power,and high radiation and because of its self-repairing capability of the radiation-damages; ii) Its synthesis technologies and growth mechanisms,including but not limited to magnetron sputtering,plasma enhanced chemical vapor deposition,hot-wire( or hot-filament) chemical vapor deposition,photo chemical vapor deposition and electron cclotron resonance chemical vapor deposition; iii) Characterization of micro- structures and properties of nc-SiC coatings grown in different techniques.
